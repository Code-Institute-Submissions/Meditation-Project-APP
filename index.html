Meditation types

MINDFULNESS MEDITATION
This kind of meditation trains you to focus on what you are thinking or feeling at the moment. You can concentrate on your thoughts,feelings,or sensations
LOVING-KINDNESS MEDITATION
One of the most promiment meditation styles, this type allows you to focus your energy to feel the warmth of love, kindness, and other positive feelings.
TRANSCEDENTAL MEDITATION
This meditation technique promotes a state of relaxation by helping you avoid distracting thoughts and feelings
ZEN MEDITATION
Zen meditation is the practice of preparing your body and mind to be calm in order to open yourself up to exploring the nature of your being
KUNDALINI YOGA
The goal of Kundalini yoga is to increase a person's vitality and consiousness
SPIRITUAL MEDITATION
This type of meditation allow your inner self to connect to something deeper than your being. It lets you reflect on your own dealings until you become fully aware of your spiritual being
